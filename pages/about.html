Elizabeth Nguyen also known by her stage name Playgirl Carti, is an American rapper and songwriter. Formerly signed to Atlanta-based underground label Awful Records, she is currently signed to A$AP Mob's AWGE Label and Interscope Records. Her eponymous debut mixtape was released on April 14, 2017, and her debut studio album Die Lit was released on May 11, 2018.
